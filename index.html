  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Instructions:

To make a cake, start by gathering your ingredients: typically flour, sugar, eggs, butter, baking powder, milk, and flavoring like vanilla. Preheat your oven to the required temperature, usually around 350°F (175°C). In a mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy, then beat in the eggs one at a time. In a separate bowl, combine the dry ingredients—flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture, alternating with milk, until the batter is smooth. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake for about 25–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let the cake cool before removing it from the pan and frosting or serving as desired.
